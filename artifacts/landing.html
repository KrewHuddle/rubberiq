§ 01 · Problem

The shop runs on paper. It shouldn't.

Pain points · 3 01.
  1. 01

    The clipboard runs intake.

    Three pads of paper at the counter. Two whiteboards in the bay. One customer at the door waiting for a number you can't quite give them.

  2. 02

    Pricing a used tire is a guess.

    Same tread, same DOT, two different counter people, two different grades. Two different prices. The margin walks out the door.

  3. 03

    Disposal is a manila folder.

    Scrap manifests live in a drawer. The hauler shows up unannounced. When the auditor visits you turn the shop upside down to prove a tire that left last March.
